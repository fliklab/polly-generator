Once upon a time, there was a rabbit who was very good at running from the day he was born. However, he had a bad temper and he ignored the animals who couldn't run as fast as him. One warm spring day, the rabbit saw a turtle crawling slowly.

옛날 옛적에 태어날 때부터 달리기를 아주 잘하는 토끼가 있었습니다. 그러나 성질이 급해서 자기만큼 빨리 달리지 못하는 동물들을 무시했다. 어느 따뜻한 봄날, 토끼는 천천히 기어가는 거북이를 보았습니다.

(The rabbit puts his arms around and looks at the turtle as if he's pathetic) "Turtle, why are you so slow?" (The turtle turns to look at the rabbit while crawling and speaks with a weak voice) "Rabbit, don't make fun of me..." "Hehe~ You're just standing still because you're so slow, you scaredy-cat!" (Sticking his tongue out) (The turtle gets angry, puts his hands on his waist, and looks at the rabbit) "What did you say? I'm not slower than you."

(토끼는 팔짱을 끼고 한심한 듯 거북이를 바라본다) "거북이야, 왜 이렇게 느려?" (거북이가 기어가는 토끼를 돌아보며 힘없는 목소리로 말한다) "토끼야, 나 놀리지 마..." "헤헤~ 너네 너무 느려서 가만히 있는거야, 겁쟁이야. -고양이!" (혀를 내밀며) (거북이가 화를 내며 허리에 손을 얹고 토끼를 바라본다) "뭐라고 했어? 내가 너보다 느리지 않아."

(The rabbit looks at the turtle as if he's ridiculous) (When the rabbit ignores him, the turtle seems to have made up his mind) Turtle: "Then, how about a race to see who's faster?" Rabbit: "Hahaha, what did you say? With a slowpoke like you?"

(토끼는 터무니없다는 듯이 거북이를 쳐다본다) (토끼가 무시하자 거북이는 마음먹은 듯 하다) 거북이: "그럼 누가 더 빠른지 경주하는 건 어때?" 토끼: "하하하, 뭐라고 했어? 너 같은 굼벵이로?"

(The rabbit looks very confident) "Ha ha, okay, you slowpoke."

(토끼가 아주 자신만만해 보인다) "하하, 알았어, 굼벵이."

(Ready, set, go! Finally, the race began) (The rabbit runs hard, taking off his shoes and running ahead of the turtle.) (But the turtle is still crawling from the starting point.) (The rabbit looks back at the turtle and laughs) "Haha! Come on, slowpoke turtle, follow me." (The turtle crawls hard and continues to go.)

(준비, 출발, 출발! 드디어 경주 시작) (토끼는 신발을 벗고 거북이보다 앞서서 힘차게 달린다.) (하지만 거북이는 여전히 출발점에서 기어가고 있다.) (토끼는 뒤를 돌아본다. 거북이와 웃음) "하하! 어서, 굼벵이 거북이, 나를 따라와." (거북이는 열심히 기어서 계속 간다.)

(After running a little further, the rabbit looks back and the turtle is still crawling slowly.) "It will take a long time for the turtle to catch up to me." (The rabbit yawns, stretches and lies down)

(조금 더 달려간 뒤에 토끼는 뒤를 돌아보고 거북이는 여전히 천천히 기어가고 있다.) "거북이가 나를 따라잡으려면 시간이 오래 걸릴 것이다." (토끼가 하품을 하고 기지개를 펴고 눕는다)

(While the rabbit is sleeping, the turtle crawls hard and approaches the tree on the hill.) (At that time, a fairy with a magic wand appears in the rabbit's dream and says,) "Rabbit, wake up." (When the fairy wakes him up, the rabbit looks around and finds the turtle near the tree on the hill.) (Startled, the rabbit covers his mouth with one hand) "Oh my gosh, when did the turtle get up there?"

(토끼가 잠든 사이 거북이는 힘겹게 기어서 언덕 위의 나무에 접근한다.) (그때 토끼의 꿈에 요술지팡이를 든 요정이 나타나서 말한다.) "토끼야, 일어나." (요정이 깨우자 토끼는 주위를 둘러보다가 언덕 위의 나무 근처에서 거북이를 발견합니다.) (놀라서 토끼는 한 손으로 입을 가리고 있습니다.) "어머, 거북이가 언제 거기 올라갔어?"

(The turtle is surprised by the rabbit's scream) (The turtle has almost reached the tree, watching the rabbit jumping around and revealing his surprise, and laughs with one hand on the tree) "I made it. I thought I was going to lose." (Only when the turtle arrives does the rabbit arrive, leaning against the tree and panting) "Phew~ even if I hadn't fallen asleep..."

(거북이는 토끼의 비명에 놀란다) (거북이는 나무에 거의 다다랐고, 토끼가 뛰어다니며 놀람을 드러내는 것을 보고, 한 손으로 나무를 잡고 웃는다) "해냈어. 내가 지는 줄 알았어." ." (거북이가 올 때만 토끼가 나무에 기대어 숨을 헐떡이며 도착한다) "휴~ 내가 잠들지 않았어도..."

(The rabbit cries and slumps down. The turtle approaches the crying rabbit and gives him candy, saying in a cheerful voice) "Rabbit, take this. My mom said to eat it with a friend." (The rabbit looks at the turtle) "Friend? You and me?" (The turtle nods and looks at the rabbit affectionately) "Yeah~, friend!" (The rabbit and turtle become friends and go down the hill together.)

(토끼가 울고 쓰러진다. 거북이가 우는 토끼에게 다가가서 명랑한 목소리로 사탕을 준다.) "토끼야, 이거 가져가. 엄마가 친구랑 먹으라고 했어." (토끼가 거북이를 쳐다본다) "친구? 너랑 나랑?" (거북이 고개를 끄덕이며 다정하게 토끼를 바라본다) "그래~ 친구!" (토끼와 거북이는 친구가 되어 함께 언덕을 내려갑니다.)
